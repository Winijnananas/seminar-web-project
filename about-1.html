About DMCE ?

Walailak University, Nakhon Si Thammarat Province, would like to cordially invite academics, lecturers, researchers, and students to join the international seminar on “Digital Media in Challenging Era, Technology, Communication, and Frontier” on 28th -29th November 2023. The objectives of this seminar are to provide opportunities for academics in related fields to exchange ideas related to digital media study in Global and Asia, to create academic and researcher cooperation among universities, and to be a forum for exchanging knowledge, information, news, and media study.
